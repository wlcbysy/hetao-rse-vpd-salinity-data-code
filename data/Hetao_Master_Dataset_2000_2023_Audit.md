## Interpretation Note

The local 2000-2020 yearly TIFF files contain LST, NIRv, Tmax, SM, and VPD, but do not contain GPP. GPP is retained as true MOD17A2H-derived values only where the local TIFF includes the GPP band, currently 2021-2023. Long-term risk projection should therefore use covariates such as VPD and NDSI, while GPP-response modeling should filter to rows with observed GPP.
